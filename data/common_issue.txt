Common Issue and Solutions:

Issue: Late Delivery
Solution:
- Wait up to 48 hours past the estimated delivery date.
- Contact support with your order number if the item is still not delivered.
- You may be eligible for shipping fee refund or a voucher.

Issue: Received a Damaged Product
Solution:
- Take clear photos of the damaged product and the packaging.
- Submit a support request within 48 hours of delivery.
- Choose between a replacement or a full refund.
- We will respond within 24 hours with further instructions.

Issue: Order Cancellation
Solution:
- Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of placement, unless already shipped.
- Refunds will be processed immediately after successful cancellation.
- Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled.
